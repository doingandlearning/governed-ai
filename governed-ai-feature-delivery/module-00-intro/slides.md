## How to get value from this bootcamp

Three things that make the difference:

<!-- pause -->

**Bring your real constraints.** The labs are designed around realistic scenarios — they work better when you stress-test them against your actual delivery context, not a hypothetical one.

<!-- pause -->

**Ask "where should this logic live?" often.** That question surfaces more architectural insight than almost any other. We'll come back to it in every module.

<!-- pause -->

**Focus on what your team can adopt next week.** Not "this is interesting" — "this is the thing I'm changing when I get back."

<!-- end_slide -->
